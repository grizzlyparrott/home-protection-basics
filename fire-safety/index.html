Fire Safety Basics

House fires move faster than most people think. Your best protection is boring: working smoke detectors, properly placed carbon monoxide alarms, a few fire extinguishers, and a plan your family has actually heard.

This section walks through where to put detectors, how many you need, what kind of extinguishers to buy, and how to build a simple, realistic escape plan.
