Home Security Basics

Most break-ins are quick, lazy, and done by someone who wants the easiest possible target. You do not need complicated systems or expensive gear—you just need to be harder to break into than the house next door.

This section covers the fundamentals: doors, locks, windows, lighting, and camera placement. Start with the high-level guide below, then move through the specific topics.
