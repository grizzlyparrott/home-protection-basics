Renter Move-Out Safety Checklist

Moving out is when landlords go hunting for reasons to keep deposits. This checklist keeps you covered—clean, safe, documented, and protected from bogus charges.

For the move-in version, see Renter Move-In Safety Checklist.

1. Safety and Damage Checks

  • [ ] Patch small nail holes (if allowed by your lease)
  • [ ] Replace burned-out bulbs
  • [ ] Test smoke alarms and replace batteries
  • [ ] Verify windows and doors still latch securely
  • [ ] Repair loose outlet covers or switch plates
  • [ ] Check for water leaks under sinks

2. Deep Cleaning

  • [ ] Clean fridge, freezer, and oven
  • [ ] Scrub bathroom surfaces
  • [ ] Sweep, mop, and vacuum all floors
  • [ ] Dust ceiling fans and vents
  • [ ] Wipe baseboards and door frames

3. Kitchen and Appliances

  • [ ] Empty and wipe cabinets
  • [ ] Clear out under-sink storage
  • [ ] Run dishwasher cleaning cycle
  • [ ] Remove old food and clean trash bins

4. Bathroom Prep

  • [ ] Clean toilet, tub, and sink
  • [ ] Remove shower curtains and bath mats
  • [ ] Check caulking for mold
  • [ ] Empty medicine cabinet

5. Bedrooms and Living Areas

  • [ ] Remove all personal items
  • [ ] Clean closets and shelves
  • [ ] Check walls for scuffs or marks
  • [ ] Verify blinds and curtains aren’t damaged

6. Outdoors and Entryways

  • [ ] Remove trash from patios or balconies
  • [ ] Sweep porches or entryways
  • [ ] Clear personal items from storage areas
  • [ ] Verify exterior lighting works

7. Utilities and Accounts

  • [ ] Notify utility companies of move-out date
  • [ ] Photograph meter readings
  • [ ] Transfer or cancel internet service
  • [ ] Change address for mail and deliveries

8. Final Security Steps

  • [ ] Lock all windows and doors
  • [ ] Return all keys, fobs, and remotes
  • [ ] Take timestamped photos of every room after cleaning
  • [ ] Verify garage door opener functions before leaving

9. Deposit Protection

  • [ ] Keep copies of messages to/from landlord
  • [ ] Store move-out photos in cloud backup
  • [ ] Request a walk-through (if allowed in your state)
  • [ ] Keep your forwarding address updated for deposit return

Handle this list and you’ll leave the place spotless, safe, and fully documented—making it hard for anyone to pull games with your deposit.
