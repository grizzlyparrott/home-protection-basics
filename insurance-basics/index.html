Home Insurance Basics

Your house can be physically secure and still wreck you financially if you do not understand your coverage. Most people do not read their policy, do not know their deductible, and do not have any record of what they own.

This section focuses on the basics: what homeowners and renters insurance actually cover, how deductibles and limits work, what is usually excluded, and how to document your home before anything goes wrong.
