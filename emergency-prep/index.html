Emergency Prep Basics

Most “disaster prep” advice is written for people who want bunkers. This is not that. This is realistic prep for the stuff that actually happens: power outages, storms, short-term evacuations, and losing access to basic services for a few days.

This section covers home emergency kits, water and food basics, power outage prep, and how to protect important documents so a flood, fire, or storm does not turn into a paperwork nightmare.
